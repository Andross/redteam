New Product Releases

On-the-fly translation and support in 100+ languages

Moveworks’ multilingual support just got even better — now in 100+ languages including Polish, Romanian, Dutch, and more. In addition to this update, we can also translate resources, such as knowledge and forms, and stakeholder content, such as Employee Comms and SendAPI, to the employee’s language of choice on the fly. Say goodbye to manually creating resources across different languages. Speak with your Customer Success Team to learn more!

New analytics dashboard to monitor the impact of your bot

Starting today, you can access Bot Performance Insights in your Moveworks Analytics Home. It’s a dashboard for Directors of IT, Service Desk leaders, and Project Managers to monitor their Moveworks’ bot’s performance, understand its impact, and optimize their strategy in one place. Try selecting flexible date periods, filtering by department, language, location, and more! Learn more here. 

Visibility into and recommendations to improve forms with Control Center

As promised last month, the Forms view of Control Center is now available! Now, you can view every form that’s imported into the Moveworks platform, receive practical guidance and tips on how to chat-optimize forms, and much more. And, if you were using an older version of the Forms module, don’t worry. You’ve automatically been upgraded to the new version. Check out the experience here and please work with your Customer Success Team if you don’t have access!

Improved Ticket Interception with Answers available

We’ve made improvements to our algorithm to increase the number of times we reach out to employees via DM when they submit a ticket that can be answered by knowledge articles. You can expect a 55% increase in self-service from Ticket Interception without compromising precision.

More knowledge sources supported at faster speed

Information that helps employees self-serve is key to driving higher automation. We’ve added 25 external knowledge sources to the list of supported sources, bringing the total number up to 75. We’ve also increased the frequency of external answer ingestion from two weeks to one. This ensures that we’re serving up-to-date information to employees. If you would like to enable any new external knowledge bases, please send a request to support@moveworks.ai. You can access the full list of supported external knowledge bases at our support documentation site.

Further updates to Group Access with automation

Last month, we announced that employees and managers can now ask their Moveworks bot to remove them from a specific distribution list just by filing a ticket. Now, it’s possible to automate removal requests instead of just having a ticket filed. If you have an existing form, speak with your Customer Success Team about upgrading to the automation experience.

Multiple geotags supported for profile boosting

Profile boosting increases self-service and employee satisfaction by ensuring that geo-specific articles are given priority. With this update, you can now tag articles with more than one location tag. This enhancement significantly reduces the time to enable profile boosting and ongoing maintenance. Talk to your Customer Success Team to learn more.

In-bot form filling coming to Moveworks for Web

Filling out forms in Moveworks for Web has gotten even easier with in-bot form filling. It leverages modal overlays to match the experience enjoyed by users in the popular chat platforms today, such as Microsoft Teams and Slack. Any questions? Speak with your Customer Success Team!
